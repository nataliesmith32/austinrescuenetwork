Who Is This Pet?





Full Model Collection (Git)

When looking for a pet, you might be considering a small dog or a large dog. You might want a dog that is a specific color, or especially breed. In fact breed selection is probably one of the highest variables when considering a new pet. But what happens when shelters take in countless animals, often mixed? By utilizing a vectoring image classifier, a user can submit an image to the Chat Bot in order to return a classified breed of that image using trained data. The Chat Bot can also produce pets based off of a requested user input, populating multiple URL links to fit the user's request.
