Pet Features for Adoption Model


Picking A Pet - Emotional or Logic Based?



When choosing the perfect family pet, often the choice is considered to be emotional; will there be a connection between you and your next best friend? In most cases, there is always some sort of conscious or subconsious bias that influences the choices that we make, especially when considering the next four legged roommate to join us (rent free!). When considering these human bias, I wanted to review the data set that we had from the Austin shelther to see if we could weigh the labeled values in a way that could train a model to predict the features of pets that impact the length of stay in the shelter. Using a subset of dogs, I took one numeric value, cleaned the remaining labeled data, and ran it through a series of regression models. Ultimately there were restrictions due to misdistribution amoung classes, as well as a data set that would do well to be expanded.
