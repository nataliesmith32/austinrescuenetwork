Pets Impacted by Disaster





Adoption Visualizations

When considering adoptions, most often pets are the last to consider in a disaster situation - as well as the shelters that care for them. One motivation behind this data research was the recent and horrific snow storm that overtook Austin, TX. By focusing our data set on that area, not only were we able to review regular trends, but also data trends of the shelther animals during the exact time of the storm.
